Privacy Policy for Game Rocket Studio

Last Updated: March 2026

At Game Rocket Studio, your creative privacy is our priority. This policy applies to ACME Harmony and the broader ACME FX Tools suite.

1. No Data Collection
ACME Harmony is a "local-first" application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, such as your name, email address, or location.

2. Local Processing of Creative Data
All color palettes, Hex codes, and project files created within the app are stored exclusively on your local machine in the .ahm (ACME Harmony Model) or .json format. Your data never leaves your device and is never uploaded to external servers.

3. No Third-Party Tracking
We do not use any analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party SDKs that monitor your behavior or usage patterns within the application.

4. App Store Transactions
Any purchases or subscriptions are handled securely by Apple via the Mac App Store. Game Rocket Studio does not have access to your credit card information or billing details.

5. Contact Us
If you have any questions regarding this policy, please visit our support page at acmefxtools.com.
